This website was created by six third year Computing and Joint Maths and Computing students from Imperial College London as a means to present their investigation and implementation of Deep Belief Networks for their Group Project.

It contains a link to an open source implementation that people are free to use.

It also allows users to create their own DBNs and provides information to those who wish to learn more behind the mechanics of DBNs.
